About nb

nb is an annotation taking tool developed by the Haystack Group at CSAIL. Students and Faculty can use nb to annotate arbitrary PDF files online, in a collaborative fashion.

A demo being worth a thousand words, you can try nb right now, using our Sandbox

nb has been used in real classes at MIT since February 2009, and has undergone three major development cycles. Still, active research and development are being carried out by the nb team.

If you're a faculty member and that you'd like to incorporate nb in your teaching, please give it a try by creating a sample site .

if you have questions or if you've found a bug, or have a feature request: Please contact us
